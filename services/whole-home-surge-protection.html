Common questions

Is whole-home surge protection worth it?

Yes. A single lightning strike can damage thousands of dollars in electronics. Whole-home protection is a small investment for major security.

Does it protect against lightning?

It absorbs most of the surge from a lightning strike, but direct strikes may still cause damage. It significantly reduces risk.
