Whole-Home Surge Protection

Protect your electronics from power surges and lightning strikes.

Overview

Power surges can damage sensitive electronics, appliances, and even your electrical panel. Our whole-home surge protection system installs at your main panel to safeguard your entire home from external surges (like lightning) and internal surges (from large appliances).

What's included

  • Protects all electronics and appliances
  • Prevents costly damage from surges
  • Easy, one-time installation
  • Peace of mind during storms

How it works

1

Site Evaluation

2

Common questions

Do I need whole-home surge protection if I have power strips?

Power strips only protect against smaller surges and don't cover your entire home. Whole-home protection guards all devices, including hardwired ones like HVAC systems.

How long does the surge protector last?

Most units last 5-10 years depending on surge frequency. We recommend annual inspections to ensure it's still working.
