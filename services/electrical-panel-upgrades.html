Electrical Panel Upgrades

Modernize your electrical panel for increased safety and capacity.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's electrical system. If it's outdated or overloaded, it can pose a fire risk and limit your ability to use modern appliances. Our electricians specialize in upgrading panels to 200 amps or more, ensuring your system meets current code and handles your energy needs.

What's included

  • Increased electrical capacity for new appliances
  • Improved safety with modern circuit breakers
  • Reduced risk of electrical fires
  • Compliance with latest electrical codes

How it works

1

Inspection & Assessment

We evaluate your current panel, load requirements, and wiring condition to recommend the best upgrade solution.

2

Permitting & Preparation

We handle all necessary permits and prepare your home for the upgrade, ensuring minimal disruption.

3

Installation & Testing

Our team installs the new panel, connects all circuits, and thoroughly tests the system for safety and performance.

Common questions

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?

Signs include flickering lights, frequent breaker trips, use of extension cords, or if your panel is over 25 years old. Our free inspection can determine if an upgrade is needed.

How long does a panel upgrade take?

Most residential panel upgrades are completed in one day. However, complex jobs may take longer. We'll provide a timeline during the estimate.
