Common questions

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?

Flickering lights, tripping breakers, or using many high-power devices are signs. If your panel is over 25 years old, an upgrade is recommended.

How long does a panel upgrade take?

Typically 4-8 hours for a standard upgrade. We'll provide a specific timeline during your estimate.
